Liu Kang Combo Tutorial

Here are the moves that will comprise our combo. Once you get familiar with performing each on their own, please go to the full combo video to see it strung together.

Our opening move for this combo will be Dragon's Breath. Pressing F+3,3,3,3 will start a low kick chain that will result with knocking our opponent in the air.


The second move of our combo is the Kontei Slam. F+2 is all that is needed for this command. The timing may be a little tricky, but a little practice will have you doing it on a consistent basis. This move has Liu Kang slam his nunchucks into his opponent with strong downward thrust, bouncing them off of the ground to continue to be juggled.


While we have our opponent in the air, the third move, No Loyalty, is a 2-kick string used to add a little damage and prime our finishing input. use 3,4 for input.


Finally, we cancel from No Loyalty into the famous Bicycle Kick with B,F+3. This will drive the opponent back towards the corner where fighting game players hate to be. If you have enough offensive meter, pressing R1/RB will cause the Bicycle kicks to extend for a longer period of time and squeezing out that extra bit of damage. The benefit of this is you will be able to drive the enemy further into the dreaded corner. The only downside is when you land, you will not have as much space between yourself and your opponent like you would without the meter burn.

Mortal Kombat 11 is a fighting game that came out on April 23rd, 2019. It is made by Netherrealm Studios and, contrary to the title, it is the 23rd entry in a growing catelouge of games since its conception in 1992. The franchise underwent a soft reboot with 2011's "Mortal Kombat" entry which was also a period that the company was changing from Midway to Netherrealm Studios. MK 11 is a traditional toe-to-toe fighting game with a healthy roster of characters that provide many different playstyles for consumers to try. It is one of the consistent games that are played in popular game tournaments.

I chose Liu Kang as the combo example in this game for a few different reasons. The first being that he is one of my top three favorite characters, alongside Goro and Mileena. Another reason is that outside of the popular Scorpion and Sub-Zero, he is one of the most known characters since he is the primary protaganist in the majority of the entries, and his likeness is derived from one of the most influential people in modern history; Bruce Lee. Along with his appeal, he is a well rounded character with easy to learn moves. He is known as a "Rushdown" character, but he has other tools in his kit for people who may want to zone or play a different style. I enjoy his move set because of the heavy martial arts influence and his trademark sounds when performing his moves.
